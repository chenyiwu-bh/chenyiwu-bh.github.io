习题 An Introduction to Manifold - Loring W. Tu, Problem 8.4

习题 An Introduction to Manifold - Loring W. Tu, Problem 8.4

给定 $x,y$ 为 $\mathbb{R}^2$ 上的标准坐标
给定开集 $U = \mathbb{R}^2 \backslash \{(x,0) \big| x \geqslant 0\}$

$U$ 上可唯一地定义极坐标 $$ \begin{aligned} x & = r\cos{\theta} \\ y & = r\sin{\theta} & r > 0 , 0 < \theta < 2\pi \end{aligned} $$

计算 $\displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial{r}} , \frac{\partial}{\partial{\theta}}$ 表示为 $\displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial{x}} , \frac{\partial}{\partial{y}}$ 的形式

Proof.
给定 $p = (x,y) \in U$
记 $F : \mathbb{R}^2 \to (0,+\infty)\times(0,\frac{\pi}{2})$

$$ (r,\theta) = F(x,y) = \left(\sqrt{x^2+y^2},\varphi_{\arctan}(x,y)\right) $$

其中

$$ \varphi_{\arctan}(x,y) = \left\{\begin{aligned} & \arctan{\frac{y}{x}} & x > 0 , y > 0 \\ & \frac{\pi}{2} & x = 0 , y > 0 \\ & \pi + \arctan{\frac{y}{x}} & x < 0 \\ & \frac{3\pi}{2} & x = 0 , y < 0 \\ & 2\pi + \arctan{\frac{y}{x}} & x > 0 , y < 0 \\ \end{aligned}\right. $$

又记

$$ \begin{aligned} F_{\ast,p}\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial{x}}\big|_{p}\right) & = a\frac{\partial}{\partial{r}}\big|_{F(p)} + b\frac{\partial}{\partial{\theta}}\big|_{F(p)} \\ F_{\ast,p}\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial{y}}\big|_{p}\right) & = c\frac{\partial}{\partial{r}}\big|_{F(p)} + d\frac{\partial}{\partial{\theta}}\big|_{F(p)} \end{aligned} $$

则

$$ \begin{aligned} a & = F_{\ast,p}\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial{x}}\big|_{p}\right)r \\ & = \frac{\partial}{\partial{x}}(r \circ F(p))\big|_{p} & (F_{\ast,p} \text{ 的定义}) \\ & = \frac{\partial}{\partial{x}}(\sqrt{x^2+y^2})\big|_{p} \\ & = \frac{x}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}} \\\\ b & = F_{\ast,p}\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial{x}}\big|_{p}\right)\theta \\ & = \frac{\partial}{\partial{x}}(\theta \circ F(p))\big|_{p} & (F_{\ast,p} \text{ 的定义}) \\ & = \frac{\partial}{\partial{x}}\left(\varphi_{\arctan}{\frac{y}{x}}\right)\big|_{p} \\ & = \frac{-y}{x^2+y^2} \\\\ c & = F_{\ast,p}\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial{y}}\big|_{p}\right)r \\ & = \frac{\partial}{\partial{y}}(r \circ F(p))\big|_{p} & (F_{\ast,p} \text{ 的定义}) \\ & = \frac{\partial}{\partial{y}}(\sqrt{x^2+y^2})\big|_{p} \\ & = \frac{y}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}} \\\\ d & = F_{\ast,p}\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial{y}}\big|_{p}\right)\theta \\ & = \frac{\partial}{\partial{y}}(\theta \circ F(p))\big|_{p} & (F_{\ast,p} \text{ 的定义}) \\ & = \frac{\partial}{\partial{y}}\left(\varphi_{\arctan}{\frac{y}{x}}\right)\big|_{p} \\ & = \frac{x}{x^2+y^2} \end{aligned} $$

即

$$ \begin{aligned} F_{\ast,p}\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial{x}}\big|_{p}\right) & = \frac{x}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}}\frac{\partial}{\partial{r}}\big|_{F(p)} + \frac{-y}{x^2+y^2}\frac{\partial}{\partial{\theta}}\big|_{F(p)} \\ F_{\ast,p}\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial{y}}\big|_{p}\right) & = \frac{y}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}}\frac{\partial}{\partial{r}}\big|_{F(p)} + \frac{x}{x^2+y^2}\frac{\partial}{\partial{\theta}}\big|_{F(p)} \end{aligned} $$

或

$$ \begin{pmatrix} \displaystyle F_{\ast,p}\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial{x}}\big|_{p}\right) \\ \displaystyle F_{\ast,p}\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial{y}}\big|_{p}\right) \end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix} \displaystyle \frac{x}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}} & \displaystyle \frac{-y}{x^2+y^2} \\ \displaystyle \frac{y}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}} & \displaystyle \frac{x}{x^2+y^2} \end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix} \displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial{r}}\big|_{F(p)} \\ \displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial{\theta}}\big|_{F(p)} \end{pmatrix} $$

那么

$$ \begin{pmatrix} \displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial{r}}\big|_{F(p)} \\ \displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial{\theta}}\big|_{F(p)} \end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix} \displaystyle \frac{x}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}} & \displaystyle \frac{-y}{x^2+y^2} \\ \displaystyle \frac{y}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}} & \displaystyle \frac{x}{x^2+y^2} \end{pmatrix}^{-1}\begin{pmatrix} \displaystyle F_{\ast,p}\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial{x}}\big|_{p}\right) \\ \displaystyle F_{\ast,p}\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial{y}}\big|_{p}\right) \end{pmatrix} $$

故

$$ \begin{pmatrix} \displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial{r}}\big|_{F(p)} \\ \displaystyle \frac{\partial}{\partial{\theta}}\big|_{F(p)} \end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix} \displaystyle \frac{x}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}} & \displaystyle \frac{y}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}} \\ -y & x \end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix} \displaystyle F_{\ast,p}\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial{x}}\big|_{p}\right) \\ \displaystyle F_{\ast,p}\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial{y}}\big|_{p}\right) \end{pmatrix} $$

即

$$ \begin{aligned} \frac{\partial}{\partial{r}}\big|_{F(p)} & = \frac{x}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}}F_{\ast,p}\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial{x}}\big|_{p}\right) + \frac{y}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}}F_{\ast,p}\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial{y}}\big|_{p}\right) \\ \frac{\partial}{\partial{\theta}}\big|_{F(p)} & = -yF_{\ast,p}\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial{x}}\big|_{p}\right) + xF_{\ast,p}\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial{y}}\big|_{p}\right) \end{aligned} $$

或简记为

$$ \begin{aligned} \frac{\partial}{\partial{r}} & = \frac{x}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}}\frac{\partial}{\partial{x}} + \frac{y}{\sqrt{x^2+y^2}}\frac{\partial}{\partial{y}} \\ \frac{\partial}{\partial{\theta}} & = -y\frac{\partial}{\partial{x}} + x\frac{\partial}{\partial{y}} \end{aligned} $$
